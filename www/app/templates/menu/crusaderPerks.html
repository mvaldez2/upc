Crusader Perks Card is Valparaiso Universities student body’s loyalty card! You can pick one up from the UPC office or at our next event!

How it works? Come to ANY UPC event and check-in on the event page on this app (along with select athletic events)! After only 9 punches you can turn your card in for a variety of prizes!

What are the prizes? Well it depends!

  • If you turn it in at a movie, you will get a free soda & candy!
  • You’ll be entered for a monthly prize drawing in the month you turn your perks card in
  • Every turned in perks card will also enter you for a GRAND SEMESTER PRIZE!
